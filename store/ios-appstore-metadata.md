```
TransKey turns your iPhone into a translator that works everywhere - even where text can't be copied.

READ ANYTHING

REPLY IN ANY LANGUAGE
- The TransKey keyboard lives inside every chat app. Copy their message, tap "Translate" and read it right above the keys.

- Type Vietnamese with Telex, plus native layouts for Korean, Japanese (romaji + kana), Chinese pinyin, Russian, Arabic and Thai.

WORK FASTER
- Copied something? Open TransKey and one tap pastes and translates it.
- Summarize, explain or refine any text with AI.
- Voice read-aloud for results, glossary for your own terms, full history.

PLANS
- Free: 20 translations a day, all core reading features.
- Mobile / Pro subscriptions remove limits and unlock reply translate, refine and desktop access. Payment goes through your App Store account; subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled in Settings.

Privacy: photos are processed for translation only and are never stored without your consent. Privacy policy: https://transkey.app/privacy - Terms: https://transkey.app/terms
