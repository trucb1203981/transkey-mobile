# TransKey - Google Play Store Listing (ASO/SEO)

Source of truth for the Play Store listing copy. Optimized for App Store
Optimization (ASO): the **App name** and **Short description** carry the most
ranking weight on Google Play, followed by keyword presence + density in the
**Full description**. Keep copy natural - keyword stuffing hurts conversion and
can be flagged.

> v2.0.0 change: the headline new feature is the **TransKey keyboard**.
> "translate keyboard" / "translator keyboard" are high-intent search terms the
> old listing never targeted - work them into the title, short desc, and an
> early body section.

---

## Target keywords (priority order)

Primary (must appear in title or short desc):
`translate` · `translator` · `keyboard` · `AI translator` · `translate keyboard`

Secondary (weave into full description, naturally):
`camera translate` · `photo translate` · `screen translate` · `voice translator`
· `chat translator` · `message translator` · `real-time translation`
· `translate keyboard` · `AI keyboard`

Long-tail / audience (sprinkle in the body):
`Korean translator` · `Japanese translator` · `Chinese translator`
· `Vietnamese keyboard` · `learn a language` · `travel translator`
· `K-pop / anime` · `read foreign news`

Do NOT claim: `offline translation` (TransKey is online), or any language count
you can't back up.

## ASO maintenance notes

- **Title and short desc rank highest.** When you change them, run a Play Console
  "Store listing experiment" (A/B) rather than swapping blind - measure install
  conversion, not just rank.
- **Keyword density:** "translate / translator / translation" and "keyboard"
  should appear several times across the body, but only where it reads naturally.
  Current draft hits both repeatedly without stuffing.
- **Localize for top markets** (vi-VN, ko-KR, ja-JP, zh) - a localized listing
  ranks for native-language searches ("번역 키보드", "翻訳 キーボード", "bàn phím dịch").
  Reuse the keyword map per language.
- **Screenshots + feature graphic** also drive ASO conversion: lead with a shot
  of the keyboard translating inside a chat (the new hero feature), then bubble,
  camera, and screen scan.
- **No AccessibilityService disclosure** - the Smart Reply auto-paste
  (AccessibilityService) feature was REMOVED. The app no longer declares
  BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE, so the listing must NOT mention accessibility
  or carry an accessibility disclosure - the old in-listing paragraph is what
  re-triggered the prominent-disclosure review that rejected v2.0.0/2.0.1.
